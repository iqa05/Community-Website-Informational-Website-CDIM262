T R A N S P O R T A T I O N

LANGKAWI INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT (LIA):

Location: Langkawi International Airport is located in Padang Matsirat, about 15-20 minutes drive from Pantai Kok.

Domestic: The airlines that operate here include Malaysia Airlines, AirAsia, Malindo Air.

International: There are direct flights from Singapore, Thailand, and China.
