Teriang House.


Located on Kuala Teriang Bay for over 150 years, Experience Island Heritage Home offers heritage suites, cottages and rooms in a private villa in Pantai Kok. The property is just a 5-minute walk from the fisherman's jetty and offers access to a small beach. Langkawi Airport and Langkawi Cable Car are within 10 minutes' drive away, while the famous Pantai Cenang can be reached with a 20-minute drive. The property offers airport pickup services. The Heritage Home is tastefully furnished, air-conditioned suites come with a King-sized bed, wooden flooring, a desk, clothes rack, wardrobe and seating area. Each room is uniquely styled and comes with their own private bathrooms equipped with toilet and shower facility. The Teriang Blue Cottages, adjacent to the Heritage Home, consist of two self-contained cottages in a gated compound with separate entrance. About 5-minute drive away to Telanga Harbour jetty for boat ride to/from Koh Lipe Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 8.4 for a two-person trip.

Berjaya Langkawi Resort.


Set amidst a lush tropical rainforest, yet just steps away from the azure waters and a gorgeous beachfront, Berjaya Langkawi Resort is an invitation to a magical sojourn embraced by Mother Nature. Hailed as one of the best 5-star Langkawi resorts, our beachfront property offers a wide range of recreational activities such as sunset cruises, island tours, sea sports and more that will transcend you to a state of bliss. Set on the edge of tropical rainforest, Berjaya Langkawi Resort offers Malay-style chalets with private balconies and contemporary furnishings. The seafront property boasts 4 restaurants, 3 bars and a free-form outdoor pool. Free WiFi is accessible throughout the resort. Fitted with wooden decor, the spacious chalets are appointed with a flat-screen TV and a seating area. Certain units located within the rainforest while others are set above the water. The private bathroom includes a rain shower and a haridryer. A buffet spread of local and international dishes are served at Dayang Cafe. Pahn-Thai Restaurant offers fine dining and specialises in Thai cuisine. Asian delights can be enjoyed at Oriental Pearl, while Beach Restaurant overlooks the sea. Drinks and refreshments are available at the Lobby Lounge and 3 bars on site. You can relax with a pampering massage at the spa or workout at the gym while the children play at the kids' club. The staff at tour desk can help arrange island tours, yacht trips and water sports activities. Shuttle service and airport transfers are provided. Berjaya Langkawi Resort is 850 metres from Langkawi Cable Car and 1.9 km from Seven Wells Waterfall. Langkawi International Airport is a 20-minute drive away. Mutiara Burau Bay Beach Resort.


Mutiara Burau Bay Beach Resort Langkawi is a 3-star hotel located along the beautiful coast of Burau Bay amid a lush rainforest. This charming beach resort boasts excellent leisure facilities and good value-for-money accommodation. Just 15 minutes from Langkawi airport, the Mutiara is situated at the lovely foothills of Mount Mat Cincang, between Pantai Kok and the beautiful beaches of Burau Bay. The chalets of the Mutiara are spacious and offer magnificent views of the landscaped garden or sea. They are also equipped with air conditioning, an en-suite bathroom and a balcony. Guests of Mutiara Burau Bay Beach Resort Langkawi can relax with a massage and take a dip in the outdoor freshwater pool. Those looking to workout can head for the tennis courts or hire a bicycle to check out the area. Other activities such as water sports, sunset cruises, horse-riding, jungle trekking and even a leisure round of golf can easily be arranged by the friendly and attentive staff of the Mutiara. In addition, guests of the Mutiara Burau Bay can enjoy a wonderful buffet of delicious local and international cuisine at the resort’s on-site restaurant. Furthermore, guests can relax with a refreshing cocktail with live music at the stylish Seashell Beach Cafe.
